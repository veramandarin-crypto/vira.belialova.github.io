01 — Who I Am

I explore movement as a form of language.

Through hip-hop, somatic practice and visual storytelling
I work with the body as a space of memory, power and expression.

For me, movement is not choreography.
It is awareness in motion.

02 — The Practice

My background in hip-hop — years of training, cyphers and stage work — shaped my relationship with rhythm, strength and raw expression.

Somatic work deepened that connection — teaching me to listen to the body beyond performance.

Visual storytelling became a way to capture presence — not just aesthetics, but energy.

Performance
Body Work
Visual Narrative

I work at the intersection of movement and meaning — but without reducing identity to labels.
It is about presence.

03 — Why It Matters

In a world that constantly pulls attention outward,
I create spaces that bring it back to the body.

Whether on stage, in session or behind the camera —
the intention is the same:
to reconnect people with their presence.

04 — Collaboration

I collaborate with brands, artists and individuals
who value depth, authenticity and movement as transformation.

Let’s build something embodied — on stage, on screen or in real space.
